Interpreting the Predictions

These predictions were made by training a multiple linear regression model using the AP Poll data from past weeks to predict the next week's rankings based on the results of the most recent games.

The "previous_rank" column shows what each team was ranked prior to the most recent games. The "predicted_rank" column shows the output of the linear regression. The "predicted_ordinal_rank" column orders these rankings from 1 to 25, and the "actual_rank" column shows what rank each team was given in the actual AP Poll.

Therefore, the "predicted_ordinal_rank" does not indicate whether a team will drop out of the top 25, while teams with a "predicted_rank" close to or greater than 26 are predicted to drop out.
